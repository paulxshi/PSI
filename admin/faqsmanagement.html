FAQs about the examination process.

Applicants must present a valid ID, their examination permit, and two 1x1 ID photos on the day of the examination.

The examination typically lasts between 3 to 4 hours depending on the type of exam. Examinees are advised to arrive 30 minutes before the scheduled time.

The examination venue will be specified in your examination permit. Please check it carefully and plan your route in advance.

Examination results are usually released within 2 to 4 weeks after the exam date. You will be notified via email and through the portal.

Rescheduling requests must be submitted at least 5 business days before the examination date. Late requests will not be accommodated.
